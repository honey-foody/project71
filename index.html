School Life

  • Life at Rachel Carson

    In Rachel Carson there are 7-8 grades. There are a total of 8 classes the classes will be split in two days odd days and even days.Odd days will have odd numbered periods 1,3,5 and 7 while even days will have even numbered periods 2,4,6 and 8.Every day busses come at 7:10 in the morning,and the doors open at 7:10.
    First period starts at 7:30 am. During the first period the teachers will play a video for the morning news called RCtv. Each class is 90 minutes long. Students will have a 5 minute break between each class they can stop by their locker or go the restroom, when there is 30 seconds left they will play a song to let the students know that the bell is almost going to ring.
    If you come late to any class after the bell rings you will be marked tardy. There will be 4 types of lunches A, B,C and D all those lunches will be at different times and will be 30 minutes long. School will end at 2:15 pm and the busses would depart at 2:30 pm.
